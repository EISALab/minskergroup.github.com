Environmental Informatic and Systems Analysis

About EISA

The EMSA group is investigating improved methods for modeling complex environmental systems so that informed management-level decisions can be made under conditions of uncertainty. Coupling machine learning approaches with sensor data and advanced information technology, innovative and cost-effective solutions to complex environmental problems are being investigated.
